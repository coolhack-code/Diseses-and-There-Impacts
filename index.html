Malaria

what it is:

Malaria is a serious infectious disease causedby plasmodium parasites, spread to humans through the bite of infected female Anopheles mosquitoes.

Common symptoms:

Fever, Chills, Headache, Sweating, Nausea, and Fatigue. Severe cases can cause anemia, Organ failure, orDeath if untreated.

Where it's Common:

Mostly in tropical and subtropical regions, especially Africa, South Asia, and parts of Southeast Asia.

Impact:

=> Causes hundreds of thousands of daeths each year, mainly among children under 5 and pregnanat woman.

=> Leads to lost school and work days, increasing poverty and slowing economic growth.

=> Strains healthcare systems in affected countries

Prevention & Treatment:

Using mosquito nets, repellents, indoor spraying, and prompt treatment with antimalarial medicines can prevent and control the disease.
